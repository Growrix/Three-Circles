# Home Page

## Page Goal

Introduce the Three Circles brand, establish credibility quickly, and drive visitors toward learning about services or submitting an inquiry.

## Required Sections

1. Hero with concise brand promise, supporting copy, and primary Contact Us CTA.
2. Service overview band highlighting the confirmed service categories.
3. Brand values or process summary grounded in creativity, quality, integrity, and collaboration.
4. Featured portfolio preview with 2 to 4 representative projects.
5. Trust section for client confidence signals, such as process clarity or project management emphasis.
6. Final CTA banner leading to Contact Us.

## Interaction Notes

- Hero CTA routes to `/contact`.
- Secondary CTA may route to `/portfolio` or `/services`.
- Featured work cards should support hover emphasis on desktop and direct tap targets on mobile.

## Content Notes

- Messaging should distinguish residential and commercial capabilities without feeling split into two disconnected brands.
- Avoid generic marketing filler; every section should reinforce design expertise and client collaboration.
