# Privacy Retention And Compliance

## Sensitive Data Inventory

- Contact names.
- Email addresses.
- Phone numbers.
- Freeform inquiry messages.
- Optional organization and project detail fields.

## Privacy Rules

- Collect only data needed to respond to inquiries and qualify design opportunities.
- Display clear form disclosure about business follow-up use.
- Do not repurpose inquiry data for unrelated marketing without explicit consent handling.

## Retention Guidance

- Retain inquiry records only as long as operationally or legally required.
- Define archival and deletion rules before launch.
- Retention and deletion actions should be auditable.

## Compliance Note

- No specific regulatory framework is stated in the source plan. Applicable privacy and cookie rules must be confirmed based on deployment geography.
