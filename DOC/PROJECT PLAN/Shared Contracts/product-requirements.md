# Product Requirements

## Business Summary

Three Circles is an interior design business serving residential and commercial clients. The website must communicate design credibility, showcase prior work, explain services clearly, and convert interested visitors into qualified leads.

## Confirmed Inputs From Source Plan

- Services offered: Interior Design, Space Planning, Furniture Selection, Color Consultation, Project Management.
- Target audiences: Homeowners, Business Owners, Real Estate Developers, Architects.
- Brand attributes: modern, sophisticated, approachable, detail-oriented, collaborative.
- Required pages: Home, About Us, Services, Portfolio, Contact Us.

## Core Product Goals

- Present a premium but approachable brand presence.
- Make services understandable to both residential and commercial prospects.
- Use the Portfolio page to establish trust through completed work and visual storytelling.
- Turn the Contact Us page into the primary conversion path.

## MVP Scope

- Public marketing website with the five required pages.
- Structured service descriptions.
- Portfolio listing and detail-ready content model.
- Contact workflow for inquiries and consultation requests.
- Brand-consistent responsive experience across desktop and mobile.

## Explicit Non-Goals

- No client portal is defined in the source plan.
- No ecommerce checkout flow is defined in the source plan.
- No multi-tenant or franchise operating model is defined in the source plan.

## Assumptions To Track

- Assumption: internal staff need a content maintenance path for portfolio items, services, and contact details.
- Assumption: contact submissions should capture project type, timeline, budget range, and preferred follow-up method to improve lead qualification.
- Assumption: the portfolio should support multiple images per project and category tags such as residential or commercial.
- Assumption: email notification is the minimum required outbound communication for new leads.

## Success Indicators

- Visitors can understand the business offer within the Home and Services pages without hidden dependencies.
- Portfolio content is easy to browse and visually credible.
- Contact submission is low-friction, mobile-friendly, and operationally trackable.
